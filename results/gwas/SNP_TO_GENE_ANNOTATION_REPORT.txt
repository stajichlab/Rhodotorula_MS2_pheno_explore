1. Lipid homeostasis (membrane composition)

3. Transcriptional regulation (ARP6 chromatin remodeling)
4. Metabolic integration (amino acid, isoprenoid pathways)

control involves both direct carotenoid biosynthesis enzymes AND regulators
of biosynthetic capacity (lipid synthesis, protein trafficking, transcription).

  (a) A single causal variant with large effect, or
  (b) Multiple linked variants all contributing to the phenotype
