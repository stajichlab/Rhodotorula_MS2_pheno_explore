EXPECTED SCENARIOS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ✓ POSITIVE SCENARIO (40% chance):
    • ~100-200 significant features identified
    • Top 5-10 show strong effects (r > 0.4)
    • Clear mechanistic insight
    • Example: "m/z 550 metabolite explains 40% of brightness variation"

  ~ REALISTIC SCENARIO (40% chance):
    • ~500+ features pass initial threshold
    • Weak correlations (r = 0.2-0.3)
    • Suggests polygenic control (many small metabolite effects)
    • Need: interaction analysis, feature combinations

  ✗ NULL SCENARIO (20% chance):
    • Few features after FDR correction
    • Suggests color phenotypes driven by:
      - Non-metabolite factors (cell wall organization)
      - Metabolite interactions (not individual effects)
    • Need: alternative approaches (ML, systems biology)
